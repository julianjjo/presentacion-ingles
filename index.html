Google founders

In 1995 Larry Page and Sergey Brin met at Stanford University. (Larry was 22 and had completed his studies at the University of Michigan, and Sergey was 21, was in charge of the school teaching).

Santford

Google began in March 1996 as a research project by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Ph.D. students at Stanford University.

Santford Google

Originally the search engine used the Stanford website with the domain google.stanford.edu. The domain google.com is registered on September 15, 1997.

Company in the garage

They formally incorporated their company, Google, on September 4, 1998 at a friend's(Susan Wojcicki) garage in Menlo Park, California.

Firts Office

in 1999 Google is installed in the first office in Mountain View (California).
